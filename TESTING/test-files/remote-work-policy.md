# Company Policy: Remote Work Guidelines

## Effective Date
November 1, 2025

## Purpose
This policy outlines the guidelines for remote work arrangements at ACME Corporation, ensuring productivity, security, and work-life balance.

## Eligibility
- Employees must have completed 6 months of continuous service
- Demonstrated ability to work independently
- Role must be suitable for remote work
- Manager approval required

## Remote Work Schedule
- **Hybrid Option:** 2-3 days per week remote
- **Full Remote:** Available for select positions
- Core hours: 9 AM - 3 PM in employee's local timezone
- Flexible start/end times within approved hours

## Equipment & Technology
### Company Provided
- Laptop with required software
- VPN access credentials
- Collaboration tools licenses (Slack, Zoom, etc.)
- Ergonomic assessment upon request

### Employee Responsibilities
- Maintain secure home office space
- Reliable high-speed internet (minimum 25 Mbps)
- Professional background for video calls
- Equipment care and maintenance

## Security Requirements
1. **Data Protection**
   - Use company VPN for all work-related activities
   - No storage of sensitive data on personal devices
   - Encrypted file sharing only
   - Lock screen when away from workstation

2. **Communication Security**
   - Company email only for business communications
   - Two-factor authentication on all accounts
   - No public WiFi for accessing company systems

## Performance Expectations
- Availability during core business hours
- Response time: Email within 4 hours, chat within 1 hour
- Weekly 1:1 with manager
- Monthly productivity reviews
- Participate in all scheduled team meetings

## Communication Protocol
- Video on for team meetings
- Status updates in Slack
- Calendar must reflect availability
- Out-of-office notifications required

## Expense Reimbursement
Eligible for reimbursement (with receipts):
- Internet costs: up to $50/month
- Office supplies: up to $100/quarter
- Ergonomic equipment: up to $500/year (with approval)

## Termination of Remote Work
Remote work privilege may be revoked if:
- Performance standards not met
- Security policies violated
- Business needs change
- Employee request
